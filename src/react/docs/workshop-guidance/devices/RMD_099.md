# 099: Kiosk Dispositivos

## Overview

## Benefits

**Single-Purpose Use:** Kiosk Dispositivos are configured for specific tasks, such as digital signage, ticket printing, or inventory management. This ensures that the Dispositivos is used only for its intended purpose.

**Enhanced Security:** By locking down the Dispositivos to a single app or a limited set of apps, the risk of unauthorized access and misuse is significantly reduced.

**Simplified Management:** IT administrators can easily manage and update kiosk Dispositivos remotely, ensuring they are always up-to-date and compliant with organizational policies.

**Consistency:** Provides a consistent user experience across all kiosk Dispositivos, which is crucial for tasks that require uniformity.

## Drawbacks

**Limited Functionality:** Kiosk Dispositivos are restricted to specific apps and functions, which might not be suitable for all use cases.

**Initial Setup Complexity:** Setting up and configuring kiosk Dispositivos can be complex and time-consuming, requiring careful planning and execution.

**User Adaptation:** Users might need time to adapt to the restricted functionality, especially if they are used to more versatile Dispositivos.

## Impact on End Users

**Focused Use:** Users will interact with Dispositivos that are tailored for specific tasks, which can improve efficiency and reduce distractions.

**Ease of Use:** The simplified interface can make it easier for users to perform their tasks without navigating through unnecessary apps or settings.

**Training:** Users may require training to understand how to use the kiosk Dispositivos effectively and to be aware of the limitations.

## Relation to Zero Trust

**Dispositivos Compliance:** Kiosk Dispositivos are configured to comply with strict security policies, ensuring they meet the Zero Trust principle of continuous compliance.

**Least Privilege:** By restricting Dispositivos to specific apps and functions, the principle of least privilege is enforced, reducing the risk of unauthorized access.

**Continuous Auditoria:** Intune allows for continuous Auditoria and management of kiosk Dispositivos, ensuring they remain secure and compliant with organizational policies.
